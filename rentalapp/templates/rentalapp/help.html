Help Center

Need assistance? Here are answers to the most frequently asked questions.

Click on the Sign Up button in the top-right corner. Choose whether you are a landlord or a tenant, and fill in the registration form.

If you are a landlord, go to your Landlord Dashboard and click on Add Property. Fill out the property details and upload images.

As a tenant, browse available properties and click Book Now. The landlord will review your booking request and approve or reject it.

Once your booking is approved, payments can be tracked in your dashboard. RentEasy does not directly handle transactions but records payment status for your reference.

If you encounter problems with your account, go to the Contact Us page and submit a request. Our support team will get back to you.

Still need help? Contact our support team.
