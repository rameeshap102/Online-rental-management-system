Privacy Policy

At RentEasy, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our platform:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Account information (role, username, password)
  • Property details submitted by landlords
  • Booking and payment records

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for:

  • Managing your account and role (landlord or tenant)
  • Processing bookings and payments
  • Improving our services
  • Sending important updates or notifications

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. Your details may be shared only:

  • With landlords or tenants to complete bookings
  • With legal authorities when required by law
  • With trusted service providers who support our operations

5. Cookies

RentEasy may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To make such requests, please contact our support team.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our platform means you accept the updated policy.
